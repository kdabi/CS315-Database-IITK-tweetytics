App Description

In our application we have performed analysis over Twitter data feed. Our database has over 145,000 tweets. The DBMS in use in MySQL. The database consists of 2 tables- Tweets and Users linked to each other. In ur application we provide 3 forms of analysis:

  1. Tweet User distribution across the world map presented in the form of heat map
  2. A word popularity comparison model that allows users to compare the popularity of different words based on their frequency in tweets in the form of a bar chart
  3. A language popularity comparison model that allows users to compare the popularity of languages given the ISO code for the language based on the tweets' languages in the form of a bar chart

We have our backend is in Python(Django) and our front-end is a mix of HTML, CSS and Javasript. We make use of the Google-Chart APIs to present our data.
